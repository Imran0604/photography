About BUPPS

BUP Photography Society (BUPPS) is the official photography club of Bangladesh University of Professionals. More than a club, BUPPS is a creative platform where students grow as visual storytellers, professionals, and collaborators. People from different backgrounds, sharing the same passion for photography, meet here.

Vision & Mission

To build a creative student community that learns, collaborates, and tells stories through photography.



We are a community of visual storytellers at BUP, dedicated to freezing time and finding beauty in the mundane.
